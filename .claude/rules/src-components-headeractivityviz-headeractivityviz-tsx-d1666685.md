## Header Activity Visualizer
Ambient soundwave-and-mascots scene that fills the gap between the tab strip and the right-side action buttons

- [HA-01 L12] HeaderActivityViz paints the slack between .tab-bar-scroll and the right-hand action buttons (.tab-resume / .tab-config / .tab-add) into a single aria-hidden <canvas> as a pixel-art coastal scene: sky, sun/moon, weather, layered ocean with foam, textured beach with decorations, and animated Claude/Codex mascots plus subagent icons. The backing store uses dpr*cssSize with ctx scaling and imageSmoothingEnabled=false; ResizeObserver drives canvas resizing and theme/css-var mutations invalidate cached tiles. Layout comes from computeLayout(w,h): beachW = clamp(round(w*0.13), 96, 180), seaMeanY = round(h*0.6), beachShoreSlope = max(9, round(h*0.18)), waveAmpMaxPx = max(5, round(h*0.13)), beachTopYAtZero = seaMeanY - beachShoreSlope, skyHorizonY = round(h*0.45), and seaStart = max(0, round(beachW*0.55)) so the sea begins inland of beachW and covers the underwater bank zone. shoreYAt(layout, tt) rises to a dune plateau, then smoothly descends past sea level and below the canvas, so the beach disappears under water rather than ending in a vertical cliff. computeWaveCrests ramps wave amplitude from seaStart through beachW into open water. drawSea is the only water renderer for the shallow bank and open ocean: the sea polygon, crest band, sparkles, foam top row, and leading-face foam all start at seaStart, with dry-beach guards that skip columns where the wave top is at or below the sand surface. drawBeach then clips and paints tiled sand/decorations/landmarks only to the dune polygon and deliberately leaves submerged-bank water pixels from drawSea untouched, avoiding a separate flat shallow-water strip. drawShoreChop paints foam only near the actual sea-level crossing. Agent slots keep xPx, dir, homeXPx, speedPxPerS, jitterSeed, diveT, and bubble state. Active agents surf between layout.beachW+4 and the right edge. Idle homes are constrained to dry sand: dryBeachHomeMaxXPx uses DRY_BEACH_HOME_FOOT_MAX_T=0.58 and subtracts half the sprite width so mascot feet remain inland of the shoreline, makeSlotInit hashes homeXPx into that dry band, updateSlot clamps the idle target to the dry band, and drawSlots clamps the visual x and samples shoreYAt at the sprite foot x before drawing. Errored/interrupted agents dive below the wave crest and emit bubbles. Beach decoration tile placement remains clamped to dryW=max(8, round(beachW*0.6)) so small ornaments and footprints do not land underwater. Weather particles, clouds, seagulls, moon reflection, night sparkles, rain/snow, and storm effects are maintained as canvas-only animation state. Pure exported helpers are covered by HeaderActivityViz Vitest cases for hash32, makeSlotInit/dryBeachHomeMaxXPx dry placement including a shoreYAt(layout, footT) < seaMeanY invariant, withAlpha, clampPx, shoreYAt waterline/monotonic behavior, and celestialPhase; the focused file currently has 21 tests.
